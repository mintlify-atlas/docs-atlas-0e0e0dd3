## Common Support Topics

### Account Management

<Accordion title="I can't access my account">
  If you're having trouble logging in:
  1. Try the [password recovery flow](/guides/password-recovery)
  2. Verify you're using the correct username
  3. Contact support if the reset code doesn't arrive
  
  Email: mediguide05@gmail.com with subject "Account Access Issue"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I want to update my account information">
  Currently, account information updates require contacting support directly. Include:
  - Your username
  - The information you need to update
  - Reason for the update
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
  To request account deletion and data removal:
  1. Email mediguide05@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your username and registered email
  3. We'll process your request within 5 business days per LFPDPPP compliance
</Accordion>

### Medical Data Questions

<Accordion title="My health readings seem incorrect">
  If you believe your health analysis results are inaccurate:
  1. Review your entered data for any input errors
  2. Check the [understanding results guide](/guides/understanding-results)
  3. Contact support with specific details about the discrepancy
  
  <Warning>
    Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns. MediGuide provides educational information only.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I export my medical history?">
  Data export features are currently in development. Contact support to request your medical data records.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is my health data secured?">
  Review our [authentication and security features](/features/authentication) for details on data protection measures.
</Accordion>

### Technical Issues

<Accordion title="The application isn't loading properly">
  Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  2. Try a different web browser
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
  
  If issues persist, email support with:
  - Browser type and version
  - Operating system
  - Description of the issue
  - Any error messages
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I'm seeing error messages">
  When reporting errors, include:
  - The exact error message
  - What you were trying to do
  - Your browser and device information
  - Screenshots if possible
</Accordion>

## Emergency Medical Situations

<Warning>
  **CRITICAL**: MediGuide is NOT for medical emergencies. If you're experiencing a medical emergency:
  
  - **Call 911** or your local emergency number immediately
  - Go to the nearest emergency room
  - Contact your healthcare provider
  
  MediGuide provides educational health information only and is NOT a substitute for professional medical care.
</Warning>

## Privacy and Data Protection

When contacting support:

- We will never ask for your password
- We may ask for account verification (username, email)
- All support conversations are confidential
- We comply with Mexican data protection laws (LFPDPPP)

For details, see our [authentication security features](/features/authentication).
