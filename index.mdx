## Comprehensive Health Monitoring

MediGuide tracks vital health parameters to provide you with complete insights:

- **Glucose Levels** - Monitor blood sugar and detect diabetes risk
- **Blood Pressure** - Track systolic and diastolic readings with hypertension alerts
- **Oxygen Saturation** - Measure SpO₂ levels and detect hypoxemia
- **Heart Rate** - Monitor cardiac rhythm and detect arrhythmias
- **Body Temperature** - Track fever and hypothermia risks
- **Respiratory Rate** - Monitor breathing patterns
- **Body Composition** - Calculate BMI and track weight management
- **Blood Type** - Store critical medical information
